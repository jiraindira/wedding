OUR STORY

This is the story of how we met and we tried our best to illustrate it. Like they say, pictures speak a thousand words!

Oct
2011

Boy Meets Girl

Boy meets girl at a common friend's birthday party while visiting NYC. Boy noticed girl but was too shy and hungover to talk to girl. Boy decided to go get pizza instead.

Oct
2011

First Contact

Back in LA, boy is still thinking about girl and reaches out to girl through the common friend. Boy and girl become friends. Boy was constantly consulting girl on life in NYC before moving to NYC. Girl patiently answered all his questions.

April
2011

The Chase

Boy realized that he really likes girl. Girl is not ready for a relationship. Girl wants best friend, boy wants girlfriend. The chase goes on for 2 years.

April
2012

The Reverse Chase

One day girl realized that girl likes boy! Yayy!! Boy not interested in relationship. Boo!! Reverse chase goes on for 6 months.

Oct
2012

Boyfriend & Girlfriend

Boy and girl finally on the same page on being in a relationship! Boy ask girl to be his girlfriend. Girl says yes! Yayyyy!!!!

Oct
2015

The Proposal

After 3 years of being together, boy decides to propose to the girl. Boy plans for proposal at a private wine tour at Chateau Fonplegade while vacationing in Bordeaux. Boy surprises girl with a custom label wine bottle and a ring while on his knees. Boy waits almost 30 seconds for an answer (girl says it was on 5 seconds!). Girl says yes!!!

AND TODAY... WE ARE GETTING MARRIED
